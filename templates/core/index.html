About Us

Looking for a hassle-free way to sell and buy digital products online? Our web application provides a seamless platform for sellers to market and sell their digital products while allowing buyers to easily discover and purchase them effectively.

AAAS is better than other marketplaces?

AAAS is designed for Sellers to make money by selling digital products which Buyers can buy and use. Seller can upload product or can add urls (in case of APIs). The platform also provide statistical analysis for better product pricing. Seller can also create attractive Brochurses from pre-defined templates and market their product on the other marketing platforms available.

This greatly help Sellers to get more sales by targeting Buyers from other marketing platforms as well. Users can manage their activites and operations hassle-free due to intuitive interfaces.

Currently there are follwing four categories of product in the platform and more will be adding in future:

  • APIs -seller can define endpoints and testing data and Buyer can subscribe to it.
  • Templates -it improve the design while saving time & money in development .
  • Logos -professionally designed logos can enhance brand recognition and credibility.
  • Downloadable Software -provides easy access to a wide range of digital tools, improving productivity and efficiency.
